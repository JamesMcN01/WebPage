Upgrades & Cosmetics

Pilots

You can customise both your imperial and republic pilots by changing their facial features, body type, gender, voice , helmet, gloves and you may also choose if your pilot wears a flight suit or regular clothing. To apply most of these changes to your pilot you must purchase them from the main menu using glory, this is a in game currency that you earn by completing multiplayer games.

Ship cosmetics

Ship decal

Once you have collected enough glory you may change the appearance of your ship by applying one of the various paint jobs that are availabe in the main menu, you can also print logos on your ship to go with your new paint job.Logos and paint jobs are different for each faction.

Cockpit

Glory can also be used to customise your cockpit by adding bobble heads, Hologram projections of various objects and Charachters and you may also hang objects from the top of your cockpit. Cockpit cosmetics vary depending on the factions cockpit you are trying to edit.

Ship components

components

All of these components can be applied to your ship at any time as long as you have purchased them, some of them are not worth purchasing and i recommend not to apply too many of them.
