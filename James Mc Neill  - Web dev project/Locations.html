Locations

Galitan

Galitan is a remote moon in the dangerous but beautiful Ringali Nebula. The forces of the turbulent nebula have pulled the moon apart into an asteroid field of still-molten fragments.

Fostar Haven

Remote trading output. At the height of the Galactic civil war, a battle between the empire and the rebels raged through the station.

Sissubo

Planet surrounded by a debris field of salvaged imperial ships that were used for the Star Hawk project. Sissubos debris field is a threat to any capital ship that enters it.

Zavian Abyss

Hazardous area of space in the Expansion Region. Its filled with asteroids charged with electrical energy from the surrounding maelstrom, and the shipwrecks of thoses who dared to venture inside.

Nadiri Dockyards

Top secret New Republic shipyard. This is where the StarHawk battleship is being constructed.

Esseles

Home to a vital, top secret, imperial listening post.

Yavin Prime

Located in the outer rim, Yavin prime is a massive gas giant. One of its moons, Yavin 4, was home to a rebel base and remains a significant location for the New Republic.
