Multiplayer

FleetBattles

Compete in the intense 5 vs 5 ranked gamemode across multiple locations from the story, coordinate with your team and choose a well balanced ship combination, shoot down the enemy fighters then advance and destroy their support ships and then finally advance on the flagship and take out the subsystems which include shields, targeting system, tractor beams, turrets and the power systems the more subsystems you take out the easier it is to destroy the ship, You must accomplish all this while defending your own ships and preventing the enemy to advance.This gamemode is very challenging for beginners and is even more troubling when playing against experienced players that is why you must be lvl 5 before you can play this mode.

Dogfights

This game mode is very simple it is a 5 vs 5 deathmatch set in various locations from the story where the only objective is to eliminate the enemy fighters the first team to reach 30 kills is the winner, this is a very fast paced game and is very good for begginers to hoan their skills.

FleetBattles vs Ai

This mode is very similar to the regular FleetBattles mode except for a few key differences, it is not a 5 vs 5 match you can play this mode solo, with friends/random players or with ai on your team but the opposition will always be ai.This is an unranked gamemode which means it will not change your rank after a game so you should use this to practice your skills either solo, with your team or even with random players, this mode is more beneificial to newer players as they can learn how to play fairly easily without beinng annihilated by enemy players.

Mc-75

Flagship of your fleet, your team can respawn and change loadouts here, when this ship is destroyed you loose, protect it at all costs.

Nebulan B frigate

Two of these Protects your flagship from enemy forces, and can resupply friendly fighters, Both must be destroyed before the enemy can attack the flagship.

Corelian corvette

Both teams have one each at the start of a match, these ships advance towards the enemy line and deals out high damage if not taken care of, once destroyed your team will only get another when they have enough kills to advance on the enemy defences.
