Synopsis

Play this story from two perspectives as a New republic and imperial pilot in this first person flight combat experience.Join vanguard squadron who have been tasked with safeguarding the Starhawk project which is a new type of capital ship that can go toe to toe with the imperial fleet. while playing from the imperial perspective you must hunt down the star hawk project and eliminate everything and everyone connected to the program.
